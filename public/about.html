About

Stefani is an entrepreneur living in Miami. She moved to the United States and spent her late childhood and teen years in Miami at American Heritage High School. Made the choice to confront the cold and attended Babson College in Wellesley, MA. After Graduation, she joined FTI Consulting where she was introduced as a consultant to the sports industry as well as the food industry. After an amazing experience with FTI Consulting Stefani fell in love with Endlessly Organic because it combined her childhood’s passion to adulthood’s responsibilities. In order to pursue her dreams she decided to move down to Florida started a lifestyle filled with healthy habits that she loves to share. With the time she spend working on her website she realized she "needed" to learn program, therefore she enrolled in Wyncode Academy. Now with a background in business, finance, end programing the oportunities are endless.

In her free time, Stefani enjoys being outdoors, going to the beach, movies, travelling the world, and meeting new people!
